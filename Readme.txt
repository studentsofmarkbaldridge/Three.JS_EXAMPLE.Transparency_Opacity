Tranparency and Opacity in Three.js 
Part of a series of tutorial examples created for the classroom of the 
Tecnologico de Monterrey, Campus Guadalajara, 2014

Mark Baldridge

---

Right Click and Drag to Orbit scene
Left Click and Drag to Pan
Scroll Wheel, Middle Click or Two Finger Track Drag to Zoom

Transparency is demonstrated by the material of the vertical plane.
Press t key to demo Opacity change.
